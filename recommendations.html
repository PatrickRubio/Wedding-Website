OUR FAVORITE THINGS TO DO

Jungle Zipline

Looking for a bit of adventure? Check out one of the many ziplining companies on the island of Maui. We love the jungle vibes and beautiful views of Jungle Zipline and NorthShore Zipline Maui!

Sail Trilogy

Join us for our welcome event as we set sail with Trilogy! Trilogy is our favorite charter-boat companies on Maui. If you love snorkeling, make sure to check out their snorkeling excursions. Discover Molokini crater or if you’re looking for an all-day excursion, discover the island of Lana’i.

Ka'anapali Beach

Maui has a plethora of beautiful beaches to spend your days swimming, snorkeling, or simply relaxing in the sun but our favorite is Ka’anapali Beach! This beach is three miles long with crystal-clear waters and is perfect for a day in the sun.

Old Lahaina Lu'au

If you’re interested in learning more about Hawaiian culture and traditional Hawaiian grub, make sure to check out Old Lahaina Lu’au. You won’t want to miss the beautiful dancing and the delicious drinks, including the Honey-Girl! If interested, be sure to book in advance as seats fill up quickly.


OUR FAVORITE RESTAURANTS

Leoda's

Leoda's is a Maui favorite! Delicious pies and sandwiches perfect to take to the beach.

Paia Fish Market

This market offers fresh fish and generous portions. We visit Paia's Fish Market multiple times every trip!

Star Noodle

Star Noodle is a perfect Asian Fusion family-style restaurant. Be sure to visit with a hearty appetite as you'll want to taste everything!

Mama's Fish House

Mama's Fish House is an upscale restaurant on the North Shore, known for their fresh-caught seafood and ocean views. Reservations required in advance.


OUR FAVORITE PLACES TO STAY

Airbnb

AirBnb is a great alternative to traditional lodging, offering the opportunity to find the best fit for you and your party.

Kapalua Villas

Kapalua Villas are spacious, peaceful vacation rentals nestled in one of Maui's most scenic areas on the west side with beautiful ocean views and lush golf courses. Check out Kapalua, Outrigger, and KBM Resorts for listings.

Marriott Hotels

There are 12 different Marriott-associated hotels on the island of Maui. Find a hotel that fits your vacay needs!

Hyatt Hotels

Looking for something more upscale? Check out the 5 Hyatt-associated hotels on Maui; all properties have beautiful views!
