The Rubios

We met in 7th grade at Luther Burbank Middle School in digital media class and both did not know what the future would hold. What began as a close friendship quickly became something more, and the summer before high school, we went on our first date. Little did we know we would develop a love that continues to grow stronger with each passing year.

Over the last decade, we have grown up together and continue to support one another as we chase our dreams. We have been inseparable since meeting, and through every season, our love has only deepened. We cannot wait to begin this next chapter, forever best friends, and soon-to-be husband and wife.

Please join us in celebrating our love, we would be honored by your presence!
