When should I RSVP by?

We please ask you to RSVP (by mail or online) by July 15th, 2025. If you RSVP by mail, please make sure to make your meal selection through the online RSVP link. Please ensure all guests addressed in the invitation fill out the RSVP link to ensure their meal selection and Welcome Party attendance is accounted for. Cheers!

Can I bring a plus-one to the welcome party or the wedding? Are children invited?

We're keeping our guest list cozy and full of familiar faces. Please refer to your invitation as we have addressed those that are invited. As much as we love your kiddos, we are only able to accommodate children of close family, who are named on the invitation. Thank you for your understanding. Thank you for celebrating us!

What time is the Wedding? Where do I park?

The wedding ceremony will begin promptly at 4:30 PM. Please plan to arrive at least 15 minutes early. Cocktail hour will follow shortly after and lead into the reception ending at 10:00PM. Free onsite parking is available. Ubers/Lyfts after the reception may be difficult to acquire; please take this into consideration when planning your transportation.

What is the wedding dress code? Colors?

Our wedding dress code is formal attire, meaning long dresses and suits. Dress to impress! Although we will have a stunning view of the water, no beachwear allowed. We encourage you to wear bright colors (optional) and kindly ask you to avoid wearing white. Still unsure? Please refer to the "Wedding Day" section for inspiration.

Can I take pictures during the ceremony?

We are having an "unplugged ceremony" (no phones or cameras). The greatest gift you can give us is being fully present as we say "I do". We have hired professionals to capture this moment for us and we promise to share our photos and videos as soon as we receive them. After the ceremony, feel free to take as many photos and videos as you like!

What do I need to know about the Welcome Party?

Let's set sail for cocktails and appetizers! We excitedly invite you to enjoy a sunset cruise the evening before our wedding. If planning to attend, please be sure to RSVP. Bri will reach out to you to complete a waiver prior to our cruise. Please see our "Welcome Party" section for more information.

Where do I park for the Welcome Party?

Parking is available both on the slip and in the adjacent lot. The parking lot uses a QR code payment system.Arrive early to pay via the QR code (you will need to know your license plate number).

What should I wear to the Welcome Party?

The welcome party is casual and will be held on a boat! Please wear hues of blue. Aloha shirts (Hawaiian shirts), shorts, and casual dresses are encouraged. Please avoid wearing heels as we don't want anyone to lose their balance and get hurt. Please refer to the welcome party section for detailed information.
